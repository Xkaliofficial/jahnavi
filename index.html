Heyy…

Idk yeh cheez tereko kabhi milegi bhi ya nai. Shayad milegi, shayad nai. But mujhe likhna tha — sirf isliye nai ki mujhe better feel ho, but isliye ki tu deserve karti hai yeh sunna. Chahe yeh ek saal baad padhe ya kabhi nai padhe. 💗

Ik yeh mein tereko 1000th time sorry aur sab bol chuka hu — aur tu soch rahi hogi "yaar phir wahi sab"… but i just dk kyu aisa hojata hai merese. Genuinely. Mujhe khud nai pata. Aur yahi baat sabse zyada hurt karti hai — that i keep messing up with people i actually care about the most. 🥺

I am sorry I wasn't the best bestfriend you wished for. Tujhe ek aisa bestfriend chahiye tha jo hamesha hota — jo kabhi disappear nai karta — aur mein woh nai tha. Aur yeh meri failure hai, teri bilkul nai. 💔

Mein tereko kahi itna ghumane leke nai gaya — aur yeh cheez mujhe aaj bhi yaad aati hai randomly. We had so many unplanned plans that never happened aur sab meri wajah se. I genuinely wanted to. Tu believe kar ya nai — I wanted to. I wish apna bestfriendship aise end nai hua hota. I wish mein thoda better hota. Thoda more present. Thoda more there. 🌷

I am sorry for treating you the way I did at times. For the words, for the behaviour, for the times I made you feel like you weren't a priority — when you actually were. Tu better deserves karti thi aur mein woh de nai paya. Genuinely sorry for that.

🌸 🌸 🌸

I won't blame this on my health — even though meri health theek nai hai. I know I am the one who ruined everything, every relation. I don't blame this on anyone — it's completely on me. Apna friendship bhi ruin hua… and trust me, I think about that more than you'd ever know. 💗

I tried very hard to keep my health intact. Idk sugar kitne time se chord diya — mujhe yaad bhi nai last time kab samosa khaya tha. Mera favourite tha woh. Ya woh hocco choco block ice cream 🍦… woh bhi bahut miss karta hu honestly. Chhoti chhoti cheezein hain jo remind karti hain ki kitna kuch change hogaya. Health ki wajah se bahut kuch nai de paya — time, energy, presence — but mujhe pata hai yeh excuse nai hai. Tu deserve karti thi better regardless of everything.

🌸 Jo yaad aata hai 🌸

Apni jo bhi baat hoti thi — snapchat pe, voice notes mein, random late night — woh genuine thi. Real thi. Forced nai thi. Aisi dosti easily nai milti life mein. Aur mujhe pata hai yeh sab meri wajah se change hua. That's something I'll carry for a really long time. 💗

Mein jaanta hu mein tereko zyada time nai de paya — aur yeh cheez mujhe genuinely regret hoti hai. Har din. Seriously. Tu mere saath thi jab mujhe chahiye tha aur mein woh properly reciprocate nai kar paya. That's not okay aur I know that.

Maana apne beech kitna bhi jagda hua ho — kitni bhi galat baat meri taraf se aayi ho — you were, and will always be, the best BESTFRIEND I would ever ask for. No cap. Teri jaisi dost milna easy nai hota. And I was lucky to even have that — even if I didn't act like it. 💝

Please live your life to the fullest. Stay happy, stay smiling — woh smile mat jaane dena apne face se. Seriously. Tu jahan bhi ho, jaise bhi ho — bus khush reh. Itni si request hai meri. Teri khushi matter karti hai. Still. Always. 🌻
